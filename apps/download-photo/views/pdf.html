For official use: Landing conditions and arrival date

An electronic visa waiver (EVW) doesn't guarantee entry into the UK; this is decided by immigration officers at the UK border.

This EVW is for a single entry to the UK for up to 6 months.

An EVW can only be used by the person named on it.

All EVW users, including children, must have their own EVW document.

You must print out your EVW and show it when you get on your plane, train or boat and on arrival in the UK.
